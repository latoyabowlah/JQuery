Taylor Swift Caught in Nicki Minaj's VMA Outrage

Minaj calls out the MTV VMAs and “white media” for snub, racism, and conspiracy.

Apparently upset by missing out on a nomination for Video of the Year at the forthcoming MTV Video Music Awards, Nicki Minaj vented, as one does, on her Twitter account.

Fellow pop titan Taylor Swift, as one of the only two women (the other being Beyoncé for "7/11") in the running for the award, for, ironically, “Bad Blood”, took the comment personally. Minaj played innocent: Huh? U must not be reading my tweets. Didn't say a word about u. I love u just as much. But u should speak on this.

Swift acquiesced:If I win, please come up with me!! You're invited to any stage I'm ever on.

A tentative peace made, Minaj took a couple swings at what she perceives as a racist media:Nothing I said had to do with Taylor. So what jabs? White media and their tactics. So sad. That's what they want.
